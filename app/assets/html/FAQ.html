FAQ

1. What is KAP Fund?

KAP Fund is a mobile application available on Apple and Android products that allows consumers to purchase concessions directly from their smartphone.

2. How much does KAP Fund cost?

KAP Fund is free to download but charges a convenience fee for orders that are processed from your phone. The convenience fee varies based on the total purchased order and service options but will be added to the total cart value upon checkout.

3. What is my KAP Fund account?

The KAP Fund account stores your personal information including username, password, cellphone number, email and credit card data. The account information also logs receipts and allows you to review previous orders that were processed on your account.

4. How secure is KAP Fund?

KAP Fund will never share your personal information with third parties specific to the terms and conditions. Your credit card information is stored on an encrypted server that is PCI compliant. KAP Fund is audited by a PCI-certified auditor and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1, this is the most stringent level of certification available. All card numbers are encrypted on disk with AES-256.

5. Where do my orders get billed?

When you place an order through KAP Fund our mobile application bills the credit card on-file provided during the account set up process. All charges will appear on your credit card's monthly statement like any other credit card purchases. No additional charges will be placed on to your mobile phone for engaging in our services.

6. How do I get ahold of my order?

Your order will be delivered to a designated location inside the venue. This location will be selected by the user and will allow users to input their specific location (i.e. Section, Row, Seat, etc.). Ballpark Concierge is not responsible for users who submit invalid information so please be mindful upon checkout. If a delivery attempt fails because user is not in their seat upon delivery, the user will receive a text stating delivery has failed and the order can be picked up at the 'Express Window'. There is never a follow up delivery.

7. How do I select a venue?

Venue selection occurs during the initial launch of the application. GPS will select the closest venue available to the user. If the selected location is not correct, the user then will have the option to select from a list of venues that is currently using the KAP Fund App. It is important that users select the appropriate venue. KAP Fund will not be responsible for users who accidently select the incorrect venue.

8. What if I forgot my password?

If a user forgets their password they will have the option to select 'forgot password'. A new password will be emailed to the email on file for the user. The user will then be asked to put the new password in and to select a different password to continue.

9. How do I update or edit my credit card information?

Users can update or edit their account information by selecting the 'my account' icon. Once a user verifies their credentials all credit card information can be edited or updated.

10. How do I delete my KAP Fund account?

Users who wish to delete their KAP Fund account can contact our organization through the customer support page and request a termination. Users can also follow the specific guidelines of their smart-phone to delete the application from their device.
